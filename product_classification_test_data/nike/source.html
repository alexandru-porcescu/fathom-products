      The durable Nike Auralux Club Training Bag features a spacious main compartment with an interior bag to store shoes and used gear.


      Secure Compartments

      The bag features exterior zip pockets to store small items and plush-lined internal pockets to secure valuables.


      Versatile Carrying

      Dual handles and an adjustable shoulder strap offers multiple carrying options.


      Smart Separation
