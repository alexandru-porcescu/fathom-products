Rated 4 out of 5 by Anonymous from Good desk for the price. Would recommend. I purchased this desk about three weeks ago. I work from home at my computer all day and was looking for a desk to put in my office. So far I am happy with my purchase. It did come in a lot of pieces and took about 5-6 hours to put together, but I was able to do it on my own. The instructions made it simple; not confusing at all. Except I did need to ask my husband for help lifting the main top panel onto the desk because it is extremely heavy. The pros: -Nice appearance. Good color...leather top makes it look more expensive than it is -Very heavy piece of furniture that does not feel flimsy or wobbly -Drawers slide easily -Good size file drawer The cons: -Although the leather top looks nice, it bugs me a bit when it comes to functionality. It is somewhat textured and has a slight ridge where it meets the wood part of the desk...kind of annoying when trying to write on a single sheet of paper, but nothing that would prevent me from buying the desk again. Also, I accidentally spilled half a glass of water on the desk; I wiped it up within one minute but by the next day there was some bubbling on the leather. As it dried over the next few days, it did start to disappear, but never went away completely. Bummer. -It was kind of a pain getting the drawers to sit straight. This was probably due to my inexperience with putting together big furniture pieces, though. Eventually I did get them straight. -This is a big con for me: There is no hole on the top or back of the desk to slide monitor cords through. For me this was a big deal because I do not have the back of the desk facing a wall; it is facing the room. So I have a bunch of visible cords that I need to figure out how to hide. This would not be a huge deal if you are planning to put the back of the desk against the wall, though. Overall I would definitely recommend this product if you are looking for an inexpensive, functional desk. Especially if you can get it on sale. For only $179, this is a steal.

Description

Update your office with the Realspace Magellan L-desk in classic cherry, honey maple, gray or espresso. The Realspace Magellan Collection is sleek, stylish and well-equipped to handle your computer, filing and storage needs.

  • Durable leatherette inlay offers generous work and display space. 
  • Plenty of drawer space for supplies and files.  The 3 box drawers offer space to organize your manuals, software, work equipment and other accessories, while the bottom file drawer accommodates your letter- or legal-size files.
  • Keep your keyboard within reach, but out of the way with the included slide-out tray. 
  • Espresso L-shaped desk houses your computer and other essentials.  Realspace Magellan Collection L-shaped desk features a closed-door cabinet that provides an ideal place for your CPU tower or larger work supplies.
  • An office L-shaped desk gives the room an elegant, professional look. 
  • Assembly required.
