Product Detail

Materials

This premium knit cardigan is made from 100% top quality extra fine Merino wool. Extremely thin 19.5 micron fibers allow an incredibly fine knit, creating an exceptionally soft, smooth feel and refined, glossy texture. This year's version has been updated with a crewneck collar and a slightly shorter hem for a sleek, compact silhouette. A great option for any look from classic to casual.
  • 100% Wool
  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Imported
